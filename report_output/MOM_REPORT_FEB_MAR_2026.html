How to read durations

All durations below are shown as minutes and seconds (not raw seconds). For a mid-sized PI firm in LA, 2–6 minutes is often a realistic screening window; under 30 seconds is frequently misdial, hang-up, or a failed connect; 10+ minutes can mean a complex intake, language barrier, or a call that should have been triaged earlier. These are rules of thumb — not verdicts on any single call.

Definitions (plain English)

Intake failure signals — Calls where the line failed before a real conversation could happen: abandoned ring, dead air, agent silent, hang-up tags, or ultra-short unanswered-style connects. It’s not “bad leads” — it’s lost attempts that still burn capacity and skew conversion.

Projection commentary (March month-end)

  • Projected March calls (excl. tests): ~834 if the first 20 days represent the whole month.
  • Caveat: Pace can overstate if the last third of March is slower (weekends, budget caps) or understate if you run heavy weekend LSA/PPC.

Operational insights

  • Silent / dead-air risk is an intake receipt problem — not just a lead-quality problem. Fixing answer discipline often beats adding keywords.
  • “Filter mode” vs “triage mode”: teams under pressure disqualify fast; high-value messy leads (attorney-switch, complex liability) need translators, not gatekeepers only.
  • Vendor + wrong-firm noise is a tax on LA PPC — you still pay for the ring.

Recommended actions

Intake

  • Open with firm + PI + geography; branch existing clients in 10 seconds.
  • Disposition every call — kill chronic “Not Scored.”

Ops / routing

  • Staff peaks shown on heatmap; overflow top sources during 11am–2pm Pacific.
  • Vendor/medical routes to admin, not case agents.

Marketing / PPC

  • Negative keyword clusters for employment, tax, family, competitor names.
  • Watch Google Ads vs Direct: if Direct gets noisier, check unlabeled numbers and brand confusion.

Reporting / QA

  • Weekly reconcile CallRail Qualified vs CRM signed retainers.
  • Spot-check attorney-switch and symptom-confusion tags — easy upside if handled well.

Final takeaway

More raw calls is not automatically a win. For a firm at your scale in LA, the pattern here suggests the marketing engine is producing volume, while receipt + classification + salvage may be the bigger lever than another point of CPC. Preventable leaks (silent connects, missing dispositions, mixed source lines) cost more than people see on a dashboard — because they never become a row in CRM.
