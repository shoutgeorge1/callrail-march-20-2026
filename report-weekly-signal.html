Attribution Coverage (CallRail ↔ Lead Docket)

Metric Value

Attribution is based on phone + timing linkage between CallRail and Lead Docket. Not all calls can be matched due to intake data limitations. Metrics are directional.

Match Confidence Definitions: High = exact phone match within same-day window (most reliable)
Medium = exact phone match within broader time window
Low = partial or weaker match (directional only)

Key Insight:
Only ~30–35% of calls can be directly tied to signed cases. Google Ads drives the majority of matched cases, followed by GMB and Direct. A significant portion of calls cannot be linked due to missing or inconsistent phone data during intake.
